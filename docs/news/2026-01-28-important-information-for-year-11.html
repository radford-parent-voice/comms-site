Important Information for Year 11

Published: 28 Jan 2026

As we begin a new academic year, I wish to extend a warm welcome to all Year 11 parents, caregivers and students. This marks the beginning of an exciting new phase in your child’s studies, and we look forward to supporting every student as they embrace the opportunities ahead in the senior years of school.

Further information about the upcoming term will be shared shortly; however, we would like to highlight two important matters.

Staffing Update

Jeremy Hawkes, Head of Year 11, is currently on parental leave. During his absence, pastoral matters may be directed to myself - Jane.Smith@radford.act.edu.au  Matters regarding subject change enquiries can be directed to Damien Nemeth, Dean of Senior Studies - Damien.Nemeth@radford.act.edu.au

Year 11 Information Evening 

Date: Tuesday, 10 February
Time: 6:00 pm to 7:30 pm
Location: Level 2, Morison Centre

The Year 11 Information Evening will provide essential details about the BSSS, the academic program, expectations and key messages to support a successful transition into senior studies. We ask that at least one parent or caregiver attends.

Presenters on the evening will include:

  • Christopher Bradbury – Principal
  • Brigid Taylor – Deputy Principal
  • Jane Smith – Director of Senior School (Years 11-12)
  • Damien Nemeth – Dean of Senior Studies
  • Dale Casburn – Dean of Student Pathways and Strategy

We look forward to partnering with you throughout the year ahead.

Jane Smith
