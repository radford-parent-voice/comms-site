2026 Timor Travels | Additional expressions of interest sought

Published: 29 Jan 2026

Due to varying circumstances there are a few additional spaces available for students in Year 11 & 12 to join our 2026 Timor Leste trip.

If you are interested please read further for details.

Students in Year 11 & 12 2026 are invited to apply to be part of the trip to Timor Leste in 2026 where we will listen, learn and build upon existing relationships with our friends and partners in Timor Leste.

Radford College has links with: Fatunaba school located in Dare; Barry and Lina on Atauro and the community of Beloi and Biqueli; AHHA Veterans School in Same; and strong connections with the Timorese Ambassador, Her Excellency Inés Almeida and other government officials. 

We are looking forward to receiving expressions of interest so that we can start working with a group of students and their families on details of the trip and preparations.
