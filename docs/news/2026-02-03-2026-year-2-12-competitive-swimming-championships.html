2026 Year 2 - 12 Competitive Swimming Championships

Published: 03 Feb 2026

We are looking forward to our 2026 Year 2-12 Competitive Swimming Championships.

This event provides Radford competitive swimmers with a pathway opportunity to qualify and progress to Regionals, School Sport State Championships and School Sport Australia National level swimming events. 

If a Radford student is aiming for selection in either the 12&U or 13&O School Sport ACT State Swimming Team to compete at the School Sport Australia Swimming Championships, they must compete in the Radford Competitive Swimming Championships, the Belconnen Carnival / ASC Carnival, and the 12 & Under or 13 & Over School Sport ACT Championships (SSACT). 

SSACT no longer offers a ‘request for inclusion’ process for students who are unable to compete in any of these events. 

2026 Radford Year 2-12 Competitive Swimming Championships

Students who do not compete at this event will attend school as per normal. 

An EdSmart permission slip will only be sent to families of students who have completed the registration by the due date. 

The Edsmart permission slip will be sent out on Friday, 6 February. Students who have not had their Edsmart completed by their parent or carer by Monday, 9 February will not be permitted to compete. We acknowledge that this is a short timeframe for completion.

A diving waiver is included in the registration process; this corresponds with diving regulations and safety protocols set by CISAC.

Parents and carers are responsible for getting their child to CISAC and signing them in with Radford staff upon arrival. Competitors are to collect a wristband that corresponds with the diving waiver response.

Parents and carers can collect their child from CISAC at the conclusion of the event. Students who are not collected by 3:00 pm from CISAC will be transported back to the college by 3:30 pm for normal school pick up arrangements and after school co-curricular activities.

Parents and carers of Years 10 to 12 students who would like their child to return to school at the conclusion of their events, but before the end of the event, must send an email to Megan.Fritsch@radford.act.edu.au by 5:00 pm on Monday, 9 February. The email is to provide permission for the student to depart CISAC, the time they will be departing and permission to walk to Radford unsupervised. 

Years 2 to 9 students require a parent or carer to sign them out from CISAC if they wish to leave before the conclusion of the championships.

For any questions, please contact alex.timms@radford.act.edu.au
