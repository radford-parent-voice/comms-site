Collection of College-owned devices

Published: 20 Jan 2026

Dear Students, Parents and Caregivers,

We are writing to inform you of the collection arrangements for College-owned devices and associated accessories.

Collection Period
Dates: 20 January to 30 January
Time: 9:00 am – 3:00 pm
Days: Business days only
Location: IT Helpdesk, Radford College

Please refer to the attached map for directions to the IT Helpdesk.

Students are required to collect their allocated device and accessories in person from the IT Helpdesk during the above period. Parents and caregivers may collect on a student’s behalf if preferred.

Important: Parents and caregivers must complete the EdSmart Device Agreement form sent on 22 December prior to device collection. Please refer to previous communication to ensure this has been completed.

Please note: Students who are unable to collect their devices and accessories during this period may do so during the second week of Term 1 by visiting the IT Helpdesk during normal operating hours.

We encourage all students to collect their devices as scheduled to ensure they are fully prepared for the start of the term.

If you have any questions or require further clarification, please contact the IT Helpdesk at help@radford.act.edu.au.

Sincerely,
Radford IT Services
