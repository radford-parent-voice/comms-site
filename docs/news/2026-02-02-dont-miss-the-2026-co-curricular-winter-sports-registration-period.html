Don't miss the 2026 co-curricular winter sports registration period

Published: 02 Feb 2026

Registrations are open for the 2026 Radford co-curricular winter sports season. 

Winter sports on offer include basketball, football, GMYM, netball, snowsports and tennis - PK-12 Sports on offer at Radford. 

Families are asked to read the information on the Radford Sport Nexus home page and the relevant sport-specific Nexus pages carefully before registering. Each page contains program details and links to the Activity Selection Portal.

Please note the registration closing dates and grace periods for each sport.

Winter rowing information and registration will be released in Term 1 on the Rowing Nexus page.

K-2 FMS Semester 1 program registration is open and closes on Tuesday, 3 February. For further information, go to the FMS Nexus page.

For questions, contact ccsports@radford.act.edu.au.

Thank you for supporting your child’s sporting journey at Radford.
