Radford College 2026 Commencement Letters

Published: 19 Jan 2026

Dear parents and caregivers, 

We are pleased to advise that 2026 commencement letters are now available. 

Checking family and student details

Before accessing your child’s commencement letter, all families are required to check and update their family details and student medical details. This information is stored in our Student Information System (Synergetic) and must be accurate for emergency purposes.

To review your details, please click here and log in using your Radford credentials. 

At the end of the form, users are required to select one of the following options: 

  • "Information is correct", or
  • "Changes required - I'll complete the required changes in the Synergetic Community portal"

After making your selection, click Submit, and your child’s commencement letter will be automatically sent to the email address we have on record for you.

If you choose “Changes required,” you will be redirected to the Synergetic Community portal to make updates directly in the system. Please note that changes may take up to one working day to be approved and reflected in our system.

You only need to complete this process once to generate commencement letters for all children attending Radford College.

Thank you for your time and cooperation.
